Welcome to SnakeWaterGun Game;

Snake Water Gun Game is a simple yet exciting two-player game based on quick thinking and luck, similar to Rock-Paper-Scissors. The game involves three elements: Snake, Water, and Gun, each of which has a specific interaction with the other.
Snake drinks Water (Snake wins over Water).
Water douses the Gun (Water wins over Gun).
Gun shoots the Snake (Gun wins over Snake).
Players secretly select one of the three elements and reveal their choices at the same time. The winner is determined based on the predefined interactions. If both players choose the same element, the round is a tie.
